Easy Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian-American casserole made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.
This simplified version uses store-bought marinara and a ricotta mixture to make it
weeknight-friendly while keeping it delicious and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat,
    then add marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  6. Add a layer of noodles, then spread some ricotta mixture, followed by mozzarella.
  7. Repeat the layers, ending with meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
