Classic Macaroni Salad

Description

Macaroni Salad is a classic, creamy side dish that's perfect for picnics, barbecues, or a light lunch.
This simple recipe combines cooked pasta with crunchy vegetables and a tangy mayo-based dressing.
It's easy to make ahead and serve chilled.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp vinegar (white or apple cider)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to cool.
  2. In a large bowl, mix mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  3. Add the cooled pasta to the dressing.
  4. Mix in celery, bell pepper, onion, and chopped eggs if using.
  5. Stir until everything is well coated.
  6. Chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour before serving.
