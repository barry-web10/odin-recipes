Easy Indian Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken is a popular Indian dish known for its creamy tomato-based
sauce and tender pieces of marinated chicken. This simplified version brings
rich flavor with minimal effort, perfect for a comforting dinner. It pairs
well with naan or steamed rice and can be made using common pantry spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or coconut cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric,
    chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Add chicken and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the marinated
    chicken until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add 1 tbsp butter and saut√© chopped onions until soft.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Stir in the cream and return the chicken to the pan. Simmer for 5-10
    minutes, letting the flavors meld.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired. Serve hot with rice or naan.
